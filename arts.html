ARTS

The Department actively encourages arts and cultural activities as an integral part of student development. Regular events, workshops, and competitions are organized to promote creativity and participation. Students from the department have showcased their talents in various inter-department and inter-college cultural fests, earning appreciation and recognition. Arts not only nurture creativity but also build confidence, teamwork, communication skills, and a sense of cultural appreciation among Students.

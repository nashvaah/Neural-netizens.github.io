PLACEMENT CELL OF GEC IDUKKI

Career Guidance and Placement Cell facilitates and inspires students for achieving their employment, higher education and entrepreneurial aspiration. The cell supports the students in terms of placement activities, and helps students through training and career awareness programmes to make them ready for employment. With active student feedback and involvement CGPC is trying to constantly upgrade students’ skill and thereby improve their chance of getting placed. Our alumni are visible in reputed companies, and are taking up higher studies in India and abroad. The Cell shall support the recruiter’s requirement of finding employable and valuable candidates. GEC Idukki students are absorbed in reputed companies such as Infosys, TCS, CTS, UST, IBS, HCL, Wipro, Quest, Qburst, Mitsogo, Trenser, Envestnet, Analytics Quotient, Talrop, Algomox, 6D Technologies, Techmaghi, Vanenburg, Coramandel Electronics, Tata Elxsi, VVDN, Gadgeon, Larsen & Toubro, Visteon, Electrifex, Schneider electric, Bosch, TVS, MRF, Ashok Leyland, Armia, Walkaroo, TVS, OPmobility, Baker Hughes, Nhanz, Enfost, Rane. CGPC values its partnership with recruiters and Alumni, and remains committed to making the recruiting experience productive and positive.

CGPC is working on the Inspire-Aspire pathway on students' professional development. We are changing and making progress. GEC Idukki is celebrating its Silver Jubilee. We welcome all recruiters this year to start a new journey, forge new partnerships and experience GEC Idukki.

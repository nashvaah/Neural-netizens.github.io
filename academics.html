ACADEMICS

Academic Overview :Government Engineering College, Idukki Institutional Affiliations Established: 2000 under the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Govt. of Kerala Affiliated University: APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) Approved by AICTE, New Delhi

  1. Undergraduate (B.Tech) Programs
    • GEC Idukki offers a range of undergraduate engineering degrees in the following disciplines:
    • Computer Science & Engineering
    • Electrical & Electronics Engineering
    • Electronics & Communication Engineering
    • Mechanical Engineering
    • Information Technology
    • Robotics & Artificial Intelligence

    Admission: Through Kerala's KEAM exam and counseling process

  2. Postgraduate (M.Tech) Programs
    • Available specializations include:
    • Power Electronics & Control
    • Computer Science & Systems Engineering
    • Network Engineering
    • VLSI & Embedded Systems

      • Admission: Based on performance in GATE followed by counseling College

  1. Departmental Highlights
    • Mechanical Engineering: Established in 2013, with NBA accreditation until 2026, housing 10 well-equipped labs. Emphasizes project-based learning Government Engineering College Idukki
    • Computer Science & Engineering (CSE): Founded in 2000, the department boasts modern facilities (software/hardware labs, high-speed internet, smart classrooms), extracurricular opportunities (hackathons, robotics, IEEE), and is a recognized research center under KTU with links to IIT Madras Incubation Cell Government Engineering College Idukki

LIBRARY

The library of Government Engineering College, Idukki, is one of the most important learning resources for students and faculty. Situated in the scenic hills of Idukki, the library provides a peaceful and inspiring atmosphere for study and research. It is well stocked with textbooks, reference books, journals, and magazines covering all branches of engineering, science, and technology. In addition to printed materials, the library also provides access to e-resources and digital collections, enabling students to keep pace with modern advancements in engineering and related fields. The spacious reading hall offers a calm environment where students can focus on their studies without disturbance. The library plays a vital role in academic life at the college. It supports classroom learning, helps students with project and research work, and encourages them to develop the habit of independent learning. By providing access to both traditional and digital resources, the library ensures that students and faculty are always equipped with the latest knowledge and information.

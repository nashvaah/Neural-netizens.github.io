MORE ABOUT IT DEPARTMENT IN GECI
The IT Department at Government Engineering College, Idukki (GECI) has been providing quality education since 2000.
It is recognized for its strong infrastructure, dedicated faculty, and an emphasis on practical, industry-aligned learning.
Accreditation and affiliation
NBA Accreditation: The B.Tech in Information Technology program received accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) in 2023.
University affiliation: The department is affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU), which is the state's leading technological university.
Academic programs
Undergraduate: Offers a Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) program in Information Technology.
Postgraduate: The department also offers an M.Tech in Computer Network Engineering, providing students with advanced knowledge in protocol engineering, and network design and analysis.
Student engagement
Extracurricular activities: The department encourages students to participate in extracurricular and co-curricular activities, including technical fests and competitions.
Technical groups: Students can be involved with organizations like the IEEE Student Branch (IEEE SB GECI), which fosters professional development.
